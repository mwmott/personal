Hi there!

Welcome to my personal site. I'm a journalist just finishing up my time at Columbia's Graduate School of Journalism who hopes to work in the intersection of technology and journalism.

Previously to journalism school I served as a reporter and later editor in chief of City on a Hill Press, which is the newspaper of record at the University of California, Santa Cruz. There I wrote articles, edited our weekly paper and online content and helped improve the fiscal health of our organization.

Before that, I was an intern and later, paid contributor, at The Amador Ledger Dispatch, which is the paper of record for Amador County, CA. I majored in Literature at UCSC and focused on what new literature in the modern world is. At Columbia, I've focused on growing my skills digitally: learning to build web pages (like this one) and doing data journalism.
