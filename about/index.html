Hi there

Welcome to my personal site! I'm a journalist just finishing up at Columbia's Graduate School of Journalism who hopes to work in the intersection of technology and journalism.

Previously, I served as editor in chief of City on a Hill Press, the student-run newspaper of record at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I wrote articles, edited our weekly paper and online content and helped improve the fiscal health of our organization.

Before that, I interned and later contributed to The Amador Ledger Dispatch, the newspaper of record of Amador County, CA. At UCSC, I majored in Literature and focused on new literature in the modern world, and at Columbia, I've focused on building digital skills.
