Hi there!

Welcome to my personal site. I'm a journalist just finishing up at Columbia's Graduate School of Journalism. After this, I hope to work in the intersection of technology and journalism, whether in building news apps like the kind at ProPublica and The New York Times, or helping news groups take advantage of online journalism in new ways.

At Columbia, I've learned how to be a better reporter, along with the tools of web design and data journalism: For web design, I'm proficient in HTML, CSS and Javascript. In data, I'm skilled in manipulating and visualizing numbers with Excel and R.

Previously, I served as editor in chief of City on a Hill Press, the student-run newspaper of record at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Before that, I contributed to The Amador Ledger Dispatch, Amador County, California's newspaper of record.
